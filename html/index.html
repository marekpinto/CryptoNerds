Roadmap

  • Stage 1

    Community Building

    Our goal is to saturate our community with people committed to our vision of a decentralized metaverse. We plan to mitigate the influence of flippers, paper hands, and FUDers. Instead, long-term holders will be well rewarded with airdrops and whitelist spots.

    We aim to consciously create a vibrant and supportive community. If you honestly engage with our community, you will be rewarded.

  • Stage 2

    Marketing, Partnerships, and Research

    We hope to give our pitch to a wide audience from various walks of life. We want to reach both people with extensive experience in the Web3 space, and those who do not even know what it is. We will only proceed with our project when we have done our due diligence in publicizing our project and reaching out to potential audiences.

    Collaboration in developing the metaverse is the cornerstone of our project, and it requires partnerships with leading names and projects in the Web3 space. As our community grows, we will reach out to relevant groups and organizations so that we can mutually assist each other, collectively benefiting us all in the long run.

    As our project progresses, we will devote large portions of our revenue to perform research on how we may best augment the development of the metaverse. As always, we will do this with the support of industry-leaders and groundbreaking projects that are appearing more and more frequently.

  • Stage 3

    The 2D CryptoNerds

    Our flagship project is the 2D CryptoNerds. The collection consists of 10,000 highly-detailed pixel art CryptoNerds with an inconceivably large number (7.5*10^105) of potential different unique avatars. Your CryptoNerd will be your ticket to our products and utilities, and it will also act as your first piece of utility (see Stage 5). The collection will also contain ten custom rare CryptoNerds, which will entitle their holders benefits.

  • Stage 4

    Third Dimension Airdrop

    After the 2D CryptoNerds sell out, we will airdrop Third Dimension Modifier NFTs (think Mutant Serum) to all CryptoNerd holders for every CryptoNerd they hold. These Modifiers can then be applied to the 2D CryptoNerds to add a final dimension to their art. The newly produced 3D rendering of the 2D NFT will then act as your interoperable avatar as we partner with leading metaverses. This rewards those who truly believe in our project and hold our NFTs.

  • Stage 5

    3D Avatar Interoperability

    Your 3D CryptoNerd avatar will act as your primary “physical” identifier within the digital world. It will be playable cross platform, and will be unique to you. Think of it as your universal ticket to the metaverse(s). Ownership of a 3D interoperable avatar will also allow you to claim various airdrops from us and our partners, and will give you access to exclusive virtual events in various metaverses.

  • Stage 6

    Metaverse Seed Investments

    As we have stressed before, CryptoNerds is a community-based venture. We will allocate a significant portion of revenue raised to sponsor up-and-coming metaverse projects. The community of CryptoNerd holders will decide where this revenue will be spent through a DAO.
